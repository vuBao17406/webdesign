Welcome to Explore Vietnam

Explore Vietnam is the ultimate travel guide to experience the beauty and rich culture of Vietnam—from the vibrant charm of the South to the majestic landscapes of the North. Designed for both international and domestic travelers, our website takes you through iconic destinations like the bustling Ho Chi Minh City, the ancient town of Hoi An, the poetic Hue, the thousand-year-old capital Hanoi, and the stunning northern highlands. Immerse yourself in Vietnam's colorful festivals like Tet and the Hoi An Lantern Festival, while savoring the unique flavors of regional cuisine across the country. On the occasion of the 50th anniversary of reunification (1975–2025), join us in showcasing Vietnam's wonders to the world!
