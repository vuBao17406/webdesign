Explore Southern Vietnam

Welcome to Northern Vietnam

Southern Vietnam offers travelers a lively and open-hearted experience, blending dynamic urban life with the peaceful charm of the countryside. In Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam’s largest metropolis, explore iconic sites like Notre-Dame Cathedral, Ben Thanh Market, and the Cu Chi Tunnels. Then head south to the Mekong Delta, where floating markets, lush orchards, and welcoming locals await.
Nature lovers will be drawn to the pristine beaches and rich ecosystems of Phu Quoc and Con Dao islands. Southern cuisine is bold, flavorful, and colorful, with popular dishes like hu tieu (Southern noodle soup), banh xeo (crispy pancakes), lau mam (fermented fish hotpot), and caramelized clay pot fish.
Southern Vietnam perfectly balances the pulse of modern life with the warmth of rural traditions – offering unforgettable experiences in every corner of the region.

Southern Vietnam

Destinations

Ho Chi Minh City - A vibrant metropolis with the Independence Palace and Ben Thanh Market.

How to get there: Fly into Tan Son Nhat International Airport (SGN).

Tip: Visit the Cu Chi Tunnels for a historical experience.

Can Tho - Famous for the Cai Rang Floating Market and Ninh Kieu Wharf.

How to get there: 3-hour drive from Ho Chi Minh City.

Tip: Visit the floating market early in the morning.

Phu Quoc - A paradise island with Sao Beach and fishing villages.

How to get there: Fly into Phu Quoc International Airport (PQC).

Tip: Visit from November to March for sunny weather.

Cuisine

Com Tam - A popular dish with broken rice and grilled pork chop.

Where to try: Com Tam Ba Ghien, Ho Chi Minh City.

Tip: Try with a fried egg on top.

Bun Ca Can Tho - A flavorful fish noodle soup with tamarind broth.

Where to try: Local eateries near Ninh Kieu Wharf.

Tip: Add fresh herbs for extra flavor.

Goi Ca Trich Phu Quoc - A fresh fish salad with herbs and coconut.

Where to try: Local restaurants on Sao Beach.

Tip: Pair with a glass of local sim wine.

Hidden Gems in Southern Vietnam

Con Dao (Ba Ria - Vung Tau)

A pristine island with untouched beaches and a rich history.

How to get there: Fly from Ho Chi Minh City to Con Dao Airport (VCS).

Tip: Visit the Con Dao Prison for a historical tour.

Tan Lap Floating Village (Long An)

A peaceful village surrounded by cajuput forests, perfect for a nature escape.

How to get there: 1.5-hour drive from Ho Chi Minh City.

Tip: Take a boat tour through the forest canals.
